# Commercial Image Types Research

This note summarizes common commercial image types that appear repeatedly across product marketing, brand campaigns, ecommerce, and creative distribution channels.

## Why The Images Were Starting To Look Too Similar

Many prompt libraries over-index on:

- isolated hero packshots
- close product-on-pedestal compositions
- luxury still-life render language

These formats are useful, but they cover only one slice of commercial demand. Real brands usually need an image system with multiple asset types.

## Common Commercial Image Types

### 1. Individual / Clean Catalog Shot

Used for:

- PDP primary image
- marketplace listing
- retail catalog

Function:

- clarity
- trust
- easy comparison

### 2. Detail / Macro Shot

Used for:

- craftsmanship proof
- texture and material storytelling
- premium conversion support

Function:

- show stitching, grain, finish, fabric, controls, or gemstone cut

### 3. Scale / Comparison Shot

Used for:

- explaining size
- helping shoppers judge proportions
- reducing uncertainty

Function:

- product next to a familiar object
- hand, room, body, or packaging context

### 4. Lifestyle / In-Use Shot

Used for:

- helping the customer imagine ownership
- showing use context
- translating utility into emotion

Function:

- product in a believable scene
- on-model, on-table, in-room, in-motion, or in-hand

### 5. Group / Collection Shot

Used for:

- showing a full range
- comparing colors, sizes, or variants
- visualizing a product family

Function:

- lineup, set, assortment, gift box, bundle

### 6. Flat Lay / Laydown Story

Used for:

- editorial storytelling
- “what’s in the bag” or ingredient story
- social content

Function:

- top-down arrangement
- strong control over composition and styling

### 7. Packaging / Unboxing Reveal

Used for:

- launch assets
- gifting campaigns
- creator-style social content

Function:

- elevate packaging design
- create anticipation and ritual

### 8. Billboard / OOH Key Visual

Used for:

- brand launch
- retail window graphics
- subway, airport, or street takeover mocks

Function:

- scale the brand beyond product detail
- prioritize headline-ready composition and strong silhouette

### 9. Social Story / Vertical Ad Frame

Used for:

- paid social
- short-lived launch pushes
- story-first mobile placements

Function:

- vertical composition
- copy-safe negative space
- fast visual read

### 10. Editorial Collage / Campaign Board

Used for:

- brand mood setting
- collection reveals
- campaign direction boards

Function:

- combine product, texture, details, cropped scenes, and graphic pacing

### 11. Brochure / Catalog / Review Graphic

Used for:

- sell sheets
- brochures
- product review graphics
- internal decks

Function:

- images that need to coexist with information blocks

## What This Means For The Repo

A strong GPT-Image-2 commercial prompt library should cover at least three layers:

1. Conversion assets
2. Brand-building campaign assets
3. Distribution-specific formats

This repo already covers layer 1 and part of layer 2. The next needed expansion is layer 3:

- vertical story ads
- packaging reveal
- OOH key visuals
- editorial collage systems
- explainer / scale compositions
- brochure-ready layouts
