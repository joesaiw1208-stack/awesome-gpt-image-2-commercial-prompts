# Organic Coffee Lifestyle Pour

## Best For

- subscription landing page
- coffee brand launch creative
- PDP storytelling image

## Prompt (EN)

```text
Goal: Create a warm but modern coffee lifestyle image with strong product readability.
Asset: Paid social or landing-page visual for an organic coffee brand.
Scene: Sunlit kitchen counter with restrained ceramic props, linen texture, and a clean editorial setup.
Subject: One coffee bag or canister as the hero product beside a freshly poured cup of coffee.
Composition: Product-first lifestyle composition, enough copy space, realistic steam, tasteful supporting props only.
Lighting: Soft golden morning light with inviting warmth and premium calm.
Materials and styling: Matte packaging, ceramic cup, linen cloth, wood grain, rich coffee crema.
Constraints: No people, no readable text, no clutter, no unrealistic pour motion, no logo, no watermark.
```

## 提示词（中文）

```text
目标：生成一张既温暖又现代的咖啡生活方式图片，同时保证产品清晰可读。
资产类型：用于有机咖啡品牌的投放素材或落地页视觉。
场景：带晨光的厨房台面，少量陶瓷道具、亚麻纹理和干净的编辑式陈列。
主体：一包或一罐咖啡作为主产品，旁边是一杯刚刚倒好的咖啡。
构图：以产品为主的生活方式构图，预留文案空间，蒸汽真实，道具克制。
光线：柔和金色晨光，温暖、治愈，同时保持高级感。
材质与风格：哑光包装、陶瓷杯、亚麻布、木纹、丰富咖啡油脂层次。
约束：不要人物，不要可读文字，不要杂乱，不要不真实的倒液动作，不要 logo，不要水印。
```

## Why It Works

It keeps the scene sellable: warm enough for emotion, controlled enough for retail clarity.
