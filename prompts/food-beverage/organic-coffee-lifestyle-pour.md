# Organic Coffee Lifestyle Pour

## Use Case

Coffee brand launch, subscription landing page, paid ad creative, and PDP storytelling image.

## Asset Type

Lifestyle beverage campaign image

## Prompt

```text
Use case: product-mockup
Asset type: coffee lifestyle campaign visual
Primary request: Create a premium e-commerce lifestyle image for an organic coffee brand with a strong artisanal but modern feel.
Scene/backdrop: warm kitchen counter scene with morning light, restrained ceramic props, linen texture, and clean editorial styling.
Subject: one coffee bag or canister as the hero product alongside a freshly poured cup of coffee, tasteful steam and realistic motion.
Style/medium: premium food and beverage commercial photography.
Composition/framing: product-first lifestyle composition, strong commercial readability, enough negative space for ad copy.
Lighting/mood: soft golden morning light, comforting, premium, inviting.
Color palette: espresso brown, oat, cream, warm beige, soft black.
Materials/textures: matte packaging, ceramic cup, linen cloth, wood grain, coffee crema.
Text (verbatim): ""
Constraints: no people, no text in image, no clutter, no unrealistic pouring motion, no watermark.
Avoid: rustic clutter overload, low-budget cafe aesthetic, chaotic farmhouse styling.
```

## Why It Works

It helps coffee brands balance warmth, craft, and product clarity without drifting into cliché.
