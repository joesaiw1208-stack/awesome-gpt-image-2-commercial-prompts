# Clean Supplement Packshot

## Use Case

Marketplace listing image, Amazon-style secondary visual, DTC supplement PDP, and conversion-focused wellness creative.

## Asset Type

Photorealistic packshot

## Prompt

```text
Use case: product-mockup
Asset type: clean supplement product image
Primary request: Create a sharp, clean e-commerce product visual for a modern wellness supplement jar designed to look premium, trustworthy, and shelf-ready.
Scene/backdrop: Minimal matte off-white studio background with a subtle grounded shadow and faint soft gradient depth.
Subject: One cylindrical supplement jar with a clean contemporary label area, white container, matte finish lid, balanced proportions, consumer wellness category.
Style/medium: commercial product photography for health and nutrition brands.
Composition/framing: straight-on front-facing packshot, centered, highly legible silhouette, generous margin around product for marketplace cropping.
Lighting/mood: bright softbox lighting, clinically clean, optimistic, modern, confidence-building.
Color palette: white, muted green, charcoal, light neutral gray.
Materials/textures: matte polymer jar, crisp printed label texture, subtle realistic shadow.
Text (verbatim): ""
Constraints: no pills spilling out, no fruits around the product, no people, no text artifacts, no watermark, no decorative clutter, no distorted label region.
Avoid: bodybuilding cliché style, aggressive sports branding, dark moody lighting, fake 3D render look.
```

## Why It Works

Supplement creative often fails because it becomes either too medical or too loud. This prompt keeps the image credible, clean, and conversion-friendly.

## Adaptation Tips

- Convert to pouch or sachet packaging
- Add ingredient-led accent colors for magnesium, collagen, greens, or probiotics
- Create a second variant with three-quarter angle for richer PDP storytelling

## Suggested Variations

- clean science-backed clinical shelf shot
- natural vitality version with restrained ingredient cues
- minimalist black-label premium nootropics version
