# Clean Supplement Packshot

## Best For

- marketplace listing
- Amazon secondary image
- DTC wellness PDP

## Prompt (EN)

```text
Goal: Create a clean supplement packshot that feels modern, credible, and shelf-ready.
Asset: Marketplace-friendly product image for a wellness brand.
Scene: Minimal off-white studio background with a grounded shadow and soft depth.
Subject: One cylindrical supplement jar with a white body, matte lid, and blank label area.
Composition: Straight-on front-facing packshot, centered, full jar visible, generous margins for marketplace crop safety.
Lighting: Bright softbox lighting, clinically clean and optimistic.
Materials and styling: Matte polymer jar, crisp edges, muted green accent band, restrained health-brand palette.
Constraints: No readable text, no pills, no fruit props, no people, no clutter, no label distortion, no watermark.
```

## 提示词（中文）

```text
目标：生成一张干净、现代、可信赖的营养补剂 packshot。
资产类型：适合 marketplace 的健康品牌产品图。
场景：极简米白色棚拍背景，带有稳定落影和柔和层次。
主体：一只圆柱形补剂罐，白色罐身、哑光盖子，标签区域保持空白。
构图：正面直拍，产品居中，整罐完整可见，四周留出足够裁切安全区。
光线：明亮柔和的柔光箱光线，整体临床感强但不冰冷。
材质与风格：哑光塑料罐、利落边缘、低饱和绿色点缀、克制健康品牌色。
约束：不要可读文字，不要药片，不要水果道具，不要人物，不要杂乱，不要标签变形，不要水印。
```

## Why It Works

It avoids both extremes that hurt wellness creative: over-medical sterility and noisy fitness-ad styling.
