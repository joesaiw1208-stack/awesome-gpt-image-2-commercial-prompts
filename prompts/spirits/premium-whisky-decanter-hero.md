# Premium Whisky Decanter Hero

## Best For

- spirits launch campaign
- gifting-season hero visual
- premium bar brand asset

## Prompt (EN)

```text
Goal: Create a premium whisky hero image that feels rich, masculine, and refined without becoming cliché.
Asset: Commercial launch visual for a luxury whisky or spirit.
Scene: Dark amber-toned studio set with smoked glass reflections and a polished stone pedestal.
Subject: One faceted whisky bottle or decanter as the hero object, partially filled with amber liquid.
Composition: Centered product with close-to-medium crop, broad shoulders clearly visible, elegant negative space.
Lighting: Warm controlled backlight and side highlights that make the liquid glow without overexposing the glass.
Materials and styling: Heavy glass, amber liquid, dark stone, subtle brass accents, premium bar atmosphere.
Constraints: No readable label text, no logo, no hands, no cigar, no ice splash clichés, no messy props, no watermark.
```

## 提示词（中文）

```text
目标：生成一张高级威士忌主视觉，气质要浓郁、成熟、精致，但不要落入俗套。
资产类型：高端烈酒发布的商业 hero 视觉。
场景：深琥珀色摄影棚环境，带烟熏玻璃反射和抛光石材底座。
主体：一只切面感明显的威士忌酒瓶或酒樽作为主角，内部有适量琥珀色酒液。
构图：居中产品，近景到中近景裁切，瓶肩轮廓清楚，留白优雅。
光线：暖色受控逆光与侧光，让酒液透亮但不过曝，玻璃高光精致。
材质与风格：厚重玻璃、琥珀酒液、深色石材、少量黄铜细节，整体像高端酒吧品牌广告。
约束：不要可读标签文字，不要 logo，不要手，不要雪茄，不要泼洒冰块老套特效，不要凌乱道具，不要水印。
```

## Why It Works

It keeps the image premium and brand-safe by replacing tired spirits tropes with material realism and controlled lighting.
