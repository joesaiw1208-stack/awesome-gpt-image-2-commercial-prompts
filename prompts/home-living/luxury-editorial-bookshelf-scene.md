# Luxury Editorial Bookshelf Scene

## Best For

- home decor campaign
- luxury lifestyle content
- editorial interior asset

## Prompt (EN)

```text
Goal: Create a luxury lifestyle interior image built around a bookshelf or shelving vignette that feels editorial and collectible.
Asset: Home-living campaign visual, editorial content asset, or interior brand story image.
Scene: Dark oak or warm walnut shelving styled with art books, sculptural objects, restrained ceramics, and gallery-like lighting.
Subject: One curated shelving vignette where objects, books, and spatial styling together become the hero rather than a single isolated SKU.
Composition: Framed interior crop with balanced asymmetry, layered shelf depth, premium negative space, and magazine-quality styling.
Lighting: Controlled gallery light or warm window light that creates rich shadows, shelf depth, and tactile highlights.
Materials and styling: Wood grain, matte paper, ceramics, brushed metal accents, restrained luxury palette, cultured editorial mood.
Constraints: No chaotic decor overload, no visible brand logos on books, no cheap showroom staging, no text overlay, no watermark.
```

## 提示词（中文）

```text
目标：生成一张围绕书架或陈列架展开的高端生活方式室内图，整体要像编辑拍摄而不是普通家居卖场图。
资产类型：家居生活 campaign 视觉、品牌内容资产或室内叙事图。
场景：深橡木或暖胡桃木书架，搭配艺术书、雕塑摆件、克制陶瓷与画廊式照明。
主体：一个经过精心策划的 shelving vignette，让书籍、摆件与空间陈列共同成为主角，而不是单一 SKU。
构图：带边界感的室内裁切，不对称但平衡，层架深度明确，留白高级，像杂志内页。
光线：受控画廊光或温暖窗光，形成丰富阴影、层架纵深和可触感高光。
材质与风格：木纹、哑光纸张、陶瓷、拉丝金属点缀、克制高奢配色、文化型编辑氛围。
约束：不要装饰过载，不要书脊出现明显品牌 logo，不要廉价样板间感，不要叠加文字，不要水印。
```
