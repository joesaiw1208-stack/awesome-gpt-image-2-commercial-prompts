# Minimal Bedding Hero

## Use Case

Bedding collection hero, catalog cover image, and sleep brand landing page visual.

## Asset Type

Interior bedding campaign image

## Prompt

```text
Use case: product-mockup
Asset type: bedding collection hero
Primary request: Create a premium minimalist bedroom campaign image focused on a luxury bedding set.
Scene/backdrop: calm modern bedroom with soft architectural lines, plaster walls, subtle daylight, and minimal decor.
Subject: one neatly styled bed with premium bedding as the clear hero product, layered texture, high-end home brand aesthetic.
Style/medium: upscale interior and catalog photography.
Composition/framing: wide hero composition with clean lines and elegant symmetry, suitable for landing page use.
Lighting/mood: soft natural morning light, calm, restorative, premium.
Color palette: warm white, sand, oat, muted stone.
Materials/textures: washed cotton, linen, upholstered headboard, matte plaster, oak accents.
Text (verbatim): ""
Constraints: no people, no pets, no text, no clutter, no distorted furniture geometry.
Avoid: generic CGI bedroom look, overly decorative styling, artificial color grading.
```

## Why It Works

It communicates comfort, material quality, and aspiration without visual overload.
