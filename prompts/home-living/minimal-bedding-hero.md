# Minimal Bedding Hero

## Best For

- bedding collection hero
- catalog cover
- sleep brand landing page

## Prompt (EN)

```text
Goal: Create a premium bedroom hero image centered on bedding quality and calm atmosphere.
Asset: Landing-page hero for a luxury bedding collection.
Scene: Minimal modern bedroom with plaster walls, soft architecture, subtle oak details, and restrained decor.
Subject: One neatly styled bed with layered bedding as the clear hero product.
Composition: Wide symmetrical composition, bed dominant, room supportive but not distracting, suitable for headline overlay.
Lighting: Soft natural morning light, calm and restorative mood.
Materials and styling: Washed cotton, linen, upholstered headboard, matte plaster, warm neutral palette.
Constraints: No people, no pets, no text, no clutter, no distorted furniture geometry, no watermark.
```

## 提示词（中文）

```text
目标：生成一张突出床品质感与宁静氛围的高端卧室主视觉。
资产类型：高端床品系列的落地页 hero 图。
场景：极简现代卧室，灰泥墙面、柔和建筑线条、少量橡木细节和克制装饰。
主体：一张整理精致的床，层叠床品是画面绝对主角。
构图：宽幅对称构图，以床为中心，空间只做陪衬，适合叠加标题文案。
光线：柔和自然晨光，氛围安静、放松、治愈。
材质与风格：水洗棉、亚麻、软包床头、哑光灰泥、暖中性色。
约束：不要人物，不要宠物，不要文字，不要杂乱，不要家具几何错误，不要水印。
```

## Why It Works

It sells comfort through material detail and room tone instead of excessive staging.
