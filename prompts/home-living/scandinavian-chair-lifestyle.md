# Scandinavian Chair Lifestyle

## Best For

- furniture PDP secondary image
- collection landing page
- catalog banner

## Prompt (EN)

```text
Goal: Create a premium furniture lifestyle image that shows scale, warmth, and product desirability.
Asset: Interior visual for a Scandinavian chair.
Scene: Sunlit room with textured plaster wall, oak floor, restrained decor, and clean modern styling.
Subject: One Scandinavian accent chair as the clear focal product.
Composition: Slightly angled room view, chair dominant, decor minimal, commercial crop-safe framing.
Lighting: Soft natural window light, serene and airy mood.
Materials and styling: Woven upholstery, oak grain, plaster texture, warm white and oat palette.
Constraints: No people, no pets, no text, no clutter, no distorted furniture geometry, no showroom CGI look, no watermark.
```

## Why It Works

It keeps the room believable without letting the environment overpower the chair.
