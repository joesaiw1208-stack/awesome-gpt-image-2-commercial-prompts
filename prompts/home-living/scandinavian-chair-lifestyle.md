# Scandinavian Chair Lifestyle

## Use Case

Furniture PDP hero, collection landing page, catalog banner, and premium home-living ad creative.

## Asset Type

Lifestyle interior product image

## Prompt

```text
Use case: product-mockup
Asset type: furniture lifestyle scene
Primary request: Create a premium interior lifestyle image centered on a Scandinavian accent chair for e-commerce and catalog use.
Scene/backdrop: calm sunlit interior with textured plaster wall, oak floor, restrained decor, and clean modern home styling.
Subject: one elegant Scandinavian accent chair as the clear hero product, realistic proportions, premium upholstery, designer appeal.
Style/medium: high-end interior photography for modern furniture brands.
Composition/framing: slightly angled room view, chair as focal point, supporting decor minimal, strong readability for commercial cropping.
Lighting/mood: soft natural window light, serene, airy, desirable, lived-in but immaculate.
Color palette: oat, oak, warm white, muted sage.
Materials/textures: woven fabric, oak wood grain, plaster texture, soft daylight shadows.
Text (verbatim): ""
Constraints: no people, no pets, no text, no clutter, no overly busy decor, no distorted furniture geometry.
Avoid: generic CGI showroom look, excessive staging, unrealistic room proportions.
```

## Why It Works

Furniture images need desire, scale, and realism. This prompt balances inspiration with product clarity.
