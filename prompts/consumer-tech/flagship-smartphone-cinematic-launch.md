# Flagship Smartphone Cinematic Launch

## Best For

- smartphone launch campaign
- keynote-style hero visual
- premium paid social creative

## Prompt (EN)

```text
Goal: Create a flagship smartphone launch image that feels cinematic, sharp, and high-status.
Asset: Commercial campaign visual for a new smartphone release.
Scene: Dark minimal studio environment with a reflective floor plane and subtle volumetric light beams.
Subject: One premium smartphone standing vertically as the hero object, with the camera module, frame, and edge profile clearly visible.
Composition: Large product presence, slightly low camera angle, symmetrical layout, strong negative space for copy.
Lighting: Controlled rim light and soft frontal fill, with refined reflections that emphasize industrial design.
Materials and styling: Satin metal frame, precision glass surfaces, restrained monochrome palette, premium technology mood.
Constraints: No logo, no readable text, no hand, no second device, no floating UI, no neon overload, no watermark.
```

## 提示词（中文）

```text
目标：生成一张旗舰手机发布视觉，画面要电影感、锐利并带有高端气场。
资产类型：新机发布的商业 campaign 图。
场景：深色极简摄影棚，带轻微反射地面和克制的体积光束。
主体：一台高端智能手机竖直站立为主角，镜头模组、金属边框和侧面轮廓都要清楚。
构图：产品占比大，略微低机位，对称布局，保留足够文案留白。
光线：受控轮廓光配合柔和正面补光，通过精致反射突出工业设计。
材质与风格：缎面金属边框、精密玻璃表面、克制单色调，整体像高端科技品牌发布会视觉。
约束：不要 logo，不要可读文字，不要手，不要第二台设备，不要悬浮 UI，不要霓虹过载，不要水印。
```

## Why It Works

It tells the model this is a commercial launch asset, so the result leans toward premium product storytelling rather than generic device rendering.
