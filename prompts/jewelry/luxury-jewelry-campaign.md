# Luxury Jewelry Campaign

## Use Case

Premium jewelry launch, social commerce ad, homepage hero, and editorial campaign image.

## Asset Type

Luxury campaign visual

## Prompt

```text
Use case: product-mockup
Asset type: jewelry campaign hero
Primary request: Create a high-luxury campaign image for a fine jewelry piece that feels like a top-tier international brand launch.
Scene/backdrop: Deep shadowy gradient backdrop with soft-focus reflective surfaces and elegant points of controlled light, minimal environment, cinematic luxury atmosphere.
Subject: One diamond necklace arranged gracefully on a dark reflective pedestal, exquisite craftsmanship, realistic gemstone sparkle, premium metal finish.
Style/medium: high-fashion jewelry photography, luxury editorial ad campaign.
Composition/framing: close-to-medium crop, subject dominant, highly intentional composition, clean negative space, upscale magazine-ad feel.
Lighting/mood: dramatic but controlled spotlighting, crisp gemstone highlights, soft falloff in the shadows, intimate, refined, exclusive.
Color palette: black, graphite, silver, white diamond brilliance.
Materials/textures: polished precious metal, finely cut gemstones, mirror-like reflection, velvet-dark background softness.
Text (verbatim): ""
Constraints: no model, no hands, no text, no logo, no exaggerated fantasy sparkle effects, no clutter, no cheap costume-jewelry look.
Avoid: bridal-catalog blandness, oversaturated glamour lighting, synthetic gems, noisy fashion set styling.
```

## Why It Works

Jewelry prompts need to balance realism and aspiration. This one cues exclusivity without tipping into artificial sparkle spam.

## Adaptation Tips

- Replace necklace with ring, bracelet, earrings, or watch
- Add brand-specific material cues such as rose gold or emerald accents
- Shift from black-luxury to champagne-editorial for softer launches

## Suggested Variations

- holiday gifting luxury version
- high-contrast noir editorial version
- rose-gold feminine premium version
