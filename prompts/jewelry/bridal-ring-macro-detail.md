# Bridal Ring Macro Detail

## Use Case

Ring PDP secondary image, luxury detail ad, and bridal collection launch asset.

## Asset Type

Jewelry macro detail image

## Prompt

```text
Use case: product-mockup
Asset type: fine jewelry macro detail
Primary request: Create a premium macro product image of a luxury engagement ring with extremely refined craftsmanship and gemstone detail.
Scene/backdrop: dark soft-focus luxury backdrop with minimal reflective surface and cinematic falloff.
Subject: one engagement ring with a brilliant center stone and elegant band, realistic metalwork and prongs, high-luxury bridal aesthetic.
Style/medium: fine jewelry macro photography.
Composition/framing: very close crop, gemstone and craftsmanship in sharp focus, elegant negative space, editorial precision.
Lighting/mood: controlled luxury spotlighting with crisp stone highlights and rich shadow depth.
Color palette: black, platinum, white brilliance.
Materials/textures: polished metal, precise gemstone facets, glossy dark reflection.
Text (verbatim): ""
Constraints: no hands, no text, no logo, no fake sparkle effects, no clutter.
Avoid: costume jewelry look, overprocessed highlights, bridal catalog blandness.
```

## Why It Works

It focuses attention on craftsmanship and cut quality, which is what premium jewelry shoppers look for.
