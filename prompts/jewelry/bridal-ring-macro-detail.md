# Bridal Ring Macro Detail

## Best For

- ring PDP detail image
- luxury retargeting creative
- bridal collection launch

## Prompt (EN)

```text
Goal: Create a macro ring image that highlights craftsmanship, cut quality, and premium finish.
Asset: PDP detail visual for a bridal ring.
Scene: Dark luxury backdrop with soft-focus falloff and a subtle reflective surface.
Subject: One engagement ring with a brilliant center stone and elegant band.
Composition: Tight macro crop with gemstone and prongs in sharp focus, strong depth, editorial precision.
Lighting: Controlled luxury spotlighting with crisp stone highlights and rich shadow depth.
Materials and styling: Polished platinum or white gold, sharp gemstone facets, dark glossy reflection, refined bridal mood.
Constraints: No hands, no text, no logo, no fake sparkle effects, no clutter, no costume-jewelry look, no watermark.
```

## 提示词（中文）

```text
目标：生成一张能突出工艺、切工和高级金属质感的戒指微距图。
资产类型：婚戒 PDP 细节展示图。
场景：深色奢华背景，带柔焦过渡和轻微反射表面。
主体：一枚带主钻和精致戒托的订婚戒指。
构图：紧凑微距构图，主钻与爪镶清晰锐利，层次感强，像编辑大片。
光线：受控奢华聚光，钻石高光干净，阴影深邃。
材质与风格：抛光铂金或白金、锋利宝石切面、深色镜面反射、精致婚嫁氛围。
约束：不要手，不要文字，不要 logo，不要假星光特效，不要杂乱，不要廉价饰品感，不要水印。
```

## Why It Works

For premium jewelry, buyers care about facets, prongs, and metal finish, so the prompt is optimized around those details.
