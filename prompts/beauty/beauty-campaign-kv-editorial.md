# Beauty Campaign KV Editorial

## Best For

- beauty launch campaign
- homepage key visual
- premium social ad

## Prompt (EN)

```text
Goal: Create a beauty campaign key visual that feels editorial, aspirational, and premium-brand ready.
Asset: Campaign KV for a luxury skincare or beauty launch.
Scene: Sculptural studio environment with one hero product, soft atmospheric set design, and elegant negative space for campaign use.
Subject: One premium beauty product as the hero object, supported by subtle styling cues that elevate brand mood without distracting from the product.
Composition: Strong hero framing, balanced but dramatic, product clearly readable, premium magazine-ad pacing, not left-heavy.
Lighting: Luminous editorial light with clean highlights, soft shadow transitions, and refined skin-care beauty polish.
Materials and styling: Frosted glass, brushed metal, creamy stone, soft haze or shadow play, quiet luxury palette.
Constraints: No logo, no dense text, no hands, no clutter, no cheap sparkle effects, no watermark.
```

## 提示词（中文）

```text
目标：生成一张高端美妆 campaign KV，让画面兼具编辑感、向往感和品牌发布气质。
资产类型：奢华护肤或美妆新品发布的 campaign 主视觉。
场景：带雕塑感的摄影棚环境，一个主产品作为核心，辅以轻量 set design 和可用于 campaign 的高级留白。
主体：一个高端美妆产品作为主角，搭配少量能抬升品牌气质的辅助线索，但不能分散产品注意力。
构图：强主视觉 framing，平衡但有戏剧性，产品清晰可读，像高级杂志广告，不要左重右轻。
光线：通透编辑光，边缘高光干净，阴影过渡柔和，有精致护肤广告的抛光感。
材质与风格：磨砂玻璃、拉丝金属、奶油石材、柔雾或光影投射、安静高奢配色。
约束：不要 logo，不要大量文字，不要手，不要杂乱，不要廉价闪光特效，不要水印。
```
