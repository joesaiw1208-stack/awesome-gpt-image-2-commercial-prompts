# Luxury Skincare Hero

## Use Case

Premium skincare launch visual for homepage hero sections, PDP banners, Meta ads, and editorial campaign assets.

## Asset Type

Photorealistic e-commerce hero image

## Prompt

```text
Use case: product-mockup
Asset type: premium skincare hero banner
Primary request: Create a luxury e-commerce campaign image for a premium facial serum bottle.
Scene/backdrop: A sculptural cream-colored stone pedestal surrounded by subtle translucent water ripples and soft floating botanical shadows, set against a warm ivory gradient background with generous negative space.
Subject: One elegant frosted glass serum bottle with a brushed metal cap, minimal unlabeled packaging, refined proportions, luxury beauty-brand aesthetic.
Style/medium: high-end commercial product photography, modern beauty editorial, globally competitive premium skincare campaign.
Composition/framing: centered hero composition, slightly low camera angle, product dominant in frame, clean breathing room around subject, balanced for website headline overlay.
Lighting/mood: soft diffused studio lighting with controlled specular highlights, luminous, clean, calm, premium, fresh.
Color palette: ivory, champagne, warm beige, transparent glass, subtle watery reflections.
Materials/textures: frosted glass, brushed aluminum, polished stone, soft water shimmer.
Text (verbatim): ""
Constraints: no logo, no watermark, no extra products, no messy splashes, no hands, no text embedded in image, no clutter, no unrealistic bottle deformation.
Avoid: low-end cosmetics ad style, oversaturated colors, plastic-looking materials, noisy background, excessive props.
```

## Why It Works

This prompt creates a premium signal through restraint: sculptural forms, controlled light, and material contrast rather than busy beauty-ad clichés.

## Adaptation Tips

- Replace serum bottle with cream jar, toner, or sunscreen tube
- Swap ivory palette for blush pink, sage, or icy silver to match the brand world
- Add a precise aspect ratio request when targeting specific ad placements

## Suggested Variations

- clinical skincare lab aesthetic
- watery glass shelf aesthetic
- black-and-gold ultra-luxury night treatment aesthetic
