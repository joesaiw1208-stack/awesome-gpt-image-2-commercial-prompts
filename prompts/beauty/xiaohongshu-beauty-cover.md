- Xiaohongshu cover
- beauty social content

## Prompt (EN)

```text
Goal: Create a high-retention Xiaohongshu beauty cover that feels premium, information-rich, and native to social sharing.
Asset: 3:4 or 4:5 cover image for a beauty post, product recommendation, or launch teaser.
Scene: Social-cover layout combining a beauty hero image, bold Chinese headline area, supporting badges or callouts, and a polished content-thumbnail structure.
Subject: One beauty-focused social cover designed to stop scrolling while still looking like a premium brand or expert account.
Composition: Vertical cover, centered hierarchy, strong title block, one dominant image area, clear accent elements, not left-heavy.
Lighting: Bright premium beauty photography in the image zone with clean, readable graphic layers.
Materials and styling: Xiaohongshu-native cover rhythm, beauty-blogger polish, Chinese typography, warm cream and blush palette with high clarity and tasteful contrast.
Constraints: No messy sticker overload, no broken Chinese text, no cheap sale-flyer feel, no clutter, no watermark.
```
