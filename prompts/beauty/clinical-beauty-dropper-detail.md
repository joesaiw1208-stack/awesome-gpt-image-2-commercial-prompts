# Clinical Beauty Dropper Detail

## Use Case

Close-up skincare campaign visual for serum ingredient storytelling, PDP sections, and social ads.

## Asset Type

Macro beauty product detail image

## Prompt

```text
Use case: product-mockup
Asset type: macro skincare detail image
Primary request: Create a premium close-up campaign image featuring a skincare dropper bottle with a suspended serum droplet.
Scene/backdrop: minimal cool-toned studio backdrop with soft frosted glass surfaces and subtle diffusion.
Subject: one elegant skincare dropper bottle and one suspended golden serum droplet, clinical-luxury beauty aesthetic.
Style/medium: commercial beauty macro photography.
Composition/framing: close crop, detail-first composition, highly controlled negative space, premium editorial feel.
Lighting/mood: soft directional light with luminous transparency and subtle highlights, clean and science-forward.
Color palette: soft white, clear glass, pale blue-gray, warm serum gold.
Materials/textures: translucent glass, glossy serum, soft frost, refined reflections.
Text (verbatim): ""
Constraints: no text, no hands, no extra products, no messy splashes, no watermark.
Avoid: cheap beauty ad tropes, neon colors, exaggerated liquid physics.
```

## Why It Works

It blends trust and luxury, which is especially useful for premium active-ingredient skincare.
