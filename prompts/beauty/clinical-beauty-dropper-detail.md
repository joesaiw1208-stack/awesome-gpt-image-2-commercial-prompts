# Clinical Beauty Dropper Detail

## Best For

- ingredient storytelling
- PDP secondary visual
- skincare social ads

## Prompt (EN)

```text
Goal: Create a clinical-luxury macro detail image for a skincare serum.
Asset: PDP secondary visual showing formula quality.
Scene: Minimal cool white studio backdrop with frosted glass surfaces and soft diffusion.
Subject: One elegant glass dropper bottle and a suspended amber serum droplet.
Composition: Tight macro crop, detail-first framing, premium editorial balance, no wasted space.
Lighting: Soft directional light, luminous transparency, clean science-forward mood.
Materials and styling: Clear glass, glossy serum, frosted surfaces, pale blue-gray neutrals, subtle reflections.
Constraints: No hands, no text, no extra products, no messy splash, no logo, no watermark, no exaggerated liquid physics.
```

## 提示词（中文）

```text
目标：生成一张兼具临床感与高级感的护肤精华微距细节图。
资产类型：用于展示配方质感的 PDP 辅助视觉。
场景：极简冷白色棚拍场景，带磨砂玻璃材质和柔和漫射背景。
主体：一只优雅的玻璃滴管瓶，以及一滴悬浮的琥珀色精华液滴。
构图：紧凑微距构图，以细节为中心，画面高级且不浪费空间。
光线：柔和方向光，强调通透感和科技感，氛围洁净理性。
材质与风格：透明玻璃、光泽液体、磨砂表面、浅蓝灰中性色、轻微倒影。
约束：不要手，不要文字，不要额外产品，不要夸张飞溅，不要 logo，不要水印，不要不真实的液体物理效果。
```

## Why It Works

This format tells the model exactly what commercial detail to emphasize: transparency, texture, and controlled luxury.
