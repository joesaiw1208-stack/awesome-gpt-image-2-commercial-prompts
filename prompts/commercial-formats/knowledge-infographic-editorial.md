# Knowledge Infographic Editorial

## Best For

- educational content asset
- trend report cover
- knowledge-rich social graphic

## Prompt (EN)

```text
Goal: Create a knowledge-dense editorial infographic image that feels designed by a real brand content team, not like a generic classroom infographic.
Asset: Educational content visual for social distribution, a report cover, or a brand knowledge post.
Scene: Clean information-design canvas combining one visual focal point with structured annotation zones, icons, mini diagrams, and modular content blocks.
Subject: One central concept, object, ingredient, or subject rendered clearly, supported by information fragments that feel curated and professional.
Composition: Dense but controlled layout, with a strong hero focal point and multiple supporting information areas arranged in a readable editorial system.
Lighting: If photographic, keep the central subject crisp and realistic; surrounding information design should integrate cleanly without visual clutter.
Materials and styling: Professional infographics, editorial graphics, brand-publishing polish, layered information hierarchy, premium content-design feel.
Constraints: No low-quality school-poster look, no random icons, no chaotic text overload, no watermark.
```

## 提示词（中文）

```text
目标：生成一张信息密度高、但依旧像专业品牌内容团队做出来的知识型信息图，而不是普通课堂海报。
资产类型：社媒知识帖、趋势报告封面图、品牌教育内容资产。
场景：干净的信息设计画布，一个视觉中心，配合结构化标注区、图标、小示意图和模块化信息块。
主体：一个核心概念、物体、成分或主题被清晰呈现，并由经过整理的信息碎片围绕说明。
构图：信息密集但受控，既有主视觉中心，也有多个辅助信息区，整体像编辑系统而不是随意拼接。
光线：如果主体偏摄影质感，要保证主体真实清晰；周围信息设计部分要干净融入，不显杂乱。
材质与风格：专业信息图、编辑设计语言、品牌出版物完成度、信息层级丰富但清楚。
约束：不要低质量课堂海报感，不要随机图标，不要文字过载杂乱，不要水印。
```
