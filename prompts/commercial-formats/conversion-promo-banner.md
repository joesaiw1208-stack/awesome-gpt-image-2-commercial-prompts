# Conversion Promo Banner

## Best For

- ecommerce sale banner
- launch promotion visual
- click-oriented paid media asset

## Prompt (EN)

```text
Goal: Create a high-conversion commercial banner that feels polished and urgent without becoming visually cheap.
Asset: Promotional banner for a launch event, limited-time offer, or ecommerce sales campaign.
Scene: Clean campaign backdrop with one hero product, strong visual hierarchy, and clear zones reserved for price, CTA, or offer copy overlays.
Subject: One lead product supported by restrained sale-oriented styling such as accent color blocking, modular cards, or offer framing.
Composition: Wide conversion-first layout, product clearly readable, negative space planned for marketing copy, immediate scannability.
Lighting: Bright, clear commercial lighting with enough contrast to make the product and promotional area stand out quickly.
Materials and styling: Performance-marketing polish, contemporary retail design, disciplined accent color, premium but conversion-focused.
Constraints: No rendered text, no logo, no clutter, no cheap discount-store look, no watermark.
```

## 提示词（中文）

```text
目标：生成一张高转化商业横幅，既要有促销效率，又不能显得廉价杂乱。
资产类型：新品活动、限时优惠或电商促销 campaign 的 banner 视觉。
场景：干净的 campaign 背景，一个主产品作为核心，并预留明确区域给价格、CTA 或活动文案叠加。
主体：一个主打产品，辅以克制的促销型设计元素，比如色块、模块卡片或优惠框架。
构图：横幅导向构图，产品清晰可读，留白为营销信息服务，一眼能扫清楚重点。
光线：明亮直接的商业布光，保证产品和促销区域在第一时间跳出来。
材质与风格：偏 performance marketing 的完成度，现代零售设计语言，有控制的强调色，兼顾高级感与转化感。
约束：不要直接生成文字，不要 logo，不要杂乱，不要廉价大促卖场感，不要水印。
```
