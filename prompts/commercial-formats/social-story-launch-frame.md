# Social Story Launch Frame

## Best For

- paid social story ad
- mobile-first launch visual
- vertical campaign frame

## Prompt (EN)

```text
Goal: Create a mobile-first commercial image designed for a vertical social story placement, with enough atmosphere to feel premium and enough structure to support copy overlays.
Asset: Vertical launch story or paid social frame.
Scene: Premium but simplified scene built around one product moment, with deliberate empty zones for headline, CTA, or pricing overlays.
Subject: One hero product or hero scene rendered clearly enough for fast mobile reading.
Composition: Tall 9:16 composition, product placed strategically off-center, strong negative space blocks, immediate readability on a phone screen.
Lighting: Bold but clean lighting that helps the subject pop even at small viewing sizes.
Materials and styling: Social-native campaign polish, clear silhouette, premium color blocking, concise visual hierarchy.
Constraints: No actual text rendered in-image, no logo, no clutter, no tiny details that disappear on mobile, no watermark.
```

## 提示词（中文）

```text
目标：生成一张专门面向竖版 Story 投放的商业图片，既有高级感，又给后续叠加文案留够结构空间。
资产类型：新品发布 Story 或付费社媒竖版广告图。
场景：围绕一个核心产品时刻搭建简洁但高级的场景，并刻意为标题、CTA 或价格信息留出空区。
主体：一个主产品或一个主场景，必须保证在手机屏幕上也能被快速识别。
构图：9:16 竖构图，产品偏心摆放，大块留白清晰，移动端一眼可读。
光线：明确但干净的布光，让主体在小屏中也足够跳出来。
材质与风格：社媒原生的 campaign 完成度，轮廓强，色块清楚，视觉层级简明。
约束：不要直接生成文字，不要 logo，不要杂乱，不要堆过多小细节，不要水印。
```
