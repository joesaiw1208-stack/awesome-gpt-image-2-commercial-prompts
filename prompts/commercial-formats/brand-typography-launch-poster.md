# Brand Typography Launch Poster

## Best For

- campaign poster
- social announcement graphic
- brand typography visual

## Prompt (EN)

```text
Goal: Create a commercial launch poster that combines strong typography, premium layout, and brand-image drama.
Asset: 4:5 social poster or campaign announcement graphic for a premium brand launch.
Scene: Bold poster composition with one dominant product or symbolic brand image, layered with clean high-end typography and disciplined spacing.
Subject: A launch announcement visual where typography and image share equal importance, like a real fashion, beauty, or lifestyle campaign poster.
Composition: Vertical poster layout, centered hierarchy, one hero image zone, one title zone, one supporting information zone, controlled graphic pacing.
Lighting: Image area should feel cinematic and polished, while the typography area stays crisp and print-like.
Materials and styling: Premium editorial poster design, sharp grid, disciplined color palette, luxury print finish, modern bilingual-friendly layout energy.
Constraints: No chaotic flyer look, no cheap gradients, no broken text, no generic meme aesthetic, no clutter, no watermark.
```

## 提示词（中文）

```text
目标：生成一张兼具强排版感、品牌高级感和商业传播力的发布海报。
资产类型：适用于 4:5 社媒海报或品牌 campaign 发布图。
场景：一个大胆但克制的海报构图，以一个主导性的产品或品牌象征图像为核心，叠加高端排版和严格留白。
主体：一张把“图像”和“文字排版”都当作主角的发布视觉，像真实时尚、美妆或生活方式品牌海报。
构图：竖版海报布局，层级居中明确，包含一个主图区域、一个标题区域和一个辅助信息区域，节奏干净。
光线：图像区域具有电影感和商业抛光感，排版区域保持清晰、印刷品般锐利。
材质与风格：高级编辑海报设计、锐利网格、克制配色、奢华印刷气质、适合双语排版的现代品牌视觉。
约束：不要混乱传单感，不要廉价渐变，不要文字损坏，不要 meme 感，不要杂乱，不要水印。
```
