# Luxury Window Display OOH

## Best For

- retail window campaign
- flagship storefront visual
- OOH luxury concept

## Prompt (EN)

```text
Goal: Create a luxury storefront window display visual that feels iconic, aspirational, and large-format campaign ready.
Asset: Retail window campaign image or storefront OOH concept for a luxury brand.
Scene: Flagship-style exterior with a dramatic display window, sculptural product staging, architectural framing, and nighttime city polish.
Subject: One luxury display window designed as a brand statement, with the merchandise or hero object staged like a collectible exhibition.
Composition: Exterior-facing composition with the window as the clear focal plane, symmetrical or elegantly balanced, premium city context, not left-heavy.
Lighting: Rich window illumination against darker surroundings, glossy reflections, controlled highlights, cinematic evening retail mood.
Materials and styling: Stone facade, brass or black metal framing, lacquered display elements, curated luxury props, flagship-store precision.
Constraints: No crowds blocking the view, no cheap mall display feel, no readable competitor logos, no clutter, no watermark.
```

## 提示词（中文）

```text
目标：生成一张高奢门店橱窗视觉，让画面具备标志性、向往感和大幅投放传播能力。
资产类型：奢侈品牌零售橱窗 campaign 图或门店 OOH 概念视觉。
场景：旗舰店气质的外立面，搭配戏剧化 display window、雕塑化产品陈列、建筑式取景和夜间城市抛光感。
主体：一个作为品牌宣言存在的 luxury display window，商品或核心物件像可收藏展陈一样摆放。
构图：面向门店外立面的构图，橱窗是绝对焦点，整体对称或优雅平衡，带少量城市语境，不要左重右轻。
光线：橱窗内部发光与外部较暗环境形成对比，带玻璃反射、受控高光和电影感夜间零售氛围。
材质与风格：石材外立面、黄铜或黑金属边框、漆面陈列装置、克制奢华道具、旗舰店级精度。
约束：不要人群挡住主视角，不要廉价商场陈列感，不要可读竞品 logo，不要杂乱，不要水印。
```
