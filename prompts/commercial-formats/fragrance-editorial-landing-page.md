# Fragrance Editorial Landing Page

## Best For

- brand landing page concept
- premium fragrance website hero
- campaign mockup screenshot

## Prompt (EN)

```text
Goal: Create a premium editorial landing page screenshot for a fragrance brand that feels modern, restrained, and commercially real.
Asset: Desktop homepage mockup or launch-page screenshot for a luxury fragrance brand.
Scene: Framed website view on a deep olive or espresso-toned background, with a cinematic hero image, refined product modules, and a clean editorial grid.
Subject: One fragrance brand homepage featuring a hero banner, selective product cards, image-led content sections, and a minimal footer system.
Composition: Tall desktop-page crop, centered framed canvas, strong vertical rhythm, abundant negative space, believable website hierarchy, polished commercial UI balance.
Lighting: Warm amber editorial imagery inside the page, with soft shadow depth and subtle screen-like realism.
Materials and styling: Minimalist ecommerce art direction, cream and stone neutrals, dark olive accents, luxe glass bottles, subtle grain, thin dividers, quiet premium typography.
Constraints: No messy UI, no generic startup dashboard look, no clutter, no excessive copy, no broken layout, no watermark.
```

## 提示词（中文）

```text
目标：生成一张高级香氛品牌落地页截图，画面要现代、克制，并且像真实可上线的商业页面。
资产类型：高端香氛品牌的桌面端官网首页 mockup / 发布页截图。
场景：深橄榄或浓咖背景中的 framed website 视图，页面内含电影感 hero 图、精简产品模块和编辑式网格。
主体：一个香氛品牌首页，包含主视觉横幅、少量产品卡片、图像主导的内容分区和极简页脚。
构图：纵向桌面页面裁切，画布居中，页面节奏清晰，留白充分，网站层级可信，整体具备商业完成度。
光线：页面内部图像以暖琥珀色编辑感光线为主，并带轻微屏幕感和柔和阴影。
材质与风格：极简电商 art direction，奶油色与石色中性基调，深橄榄强调色，奢华玻璃瓶，细腻颗粒感，细分隔线，安静高级排版。
约束：不要混乱 UI，不要通用创业公司 dashboard 感，不要杂乱，不要大量文案，不要布局错误，不要水印。
```
