# Trust Proof Case Study Visual

## Best For

- testimonial section art
- case study header image
- credibility-building landing page block

## Prompt (EN)

```text
Goal: Create a credibility-building commercial visual that supports trust, proof, and decision confidence rather than pure product beauty.
Asset: Case study cover image or trust section visual for a landing page or sales deck.
Scene: Clean professional layout environment combining product, outcome context, and subtle data-visualization or documentation cues.
Subject: One product or service context supported by proof-oriented elements such as charts, review-card placeholders, certification-style motifs, or success indicators.
Composition: Structured and editorial, with visual hierarchy that suggests evidence and professionalism, while leaving room for testimonial copy or metrics overlays.
Lighting: Clear, polished, business-friendly lighting with premium clarity and minimal drama.
Materials and styling: Modern B2B/B2C trust aesthetic, restrained palette, strong legibility, evidence-led design language.
Constraints: No fake readable reviews, no brand logos, no messy infographic overload, no watermark.
```

## 提示词（中文）

```text
目标：生成一张强化可信度和购买信心的商业视觉，重点不是单纯好看，而是让人感到“这东西值得信任”。
资产类型：落地页 trust section、案例页封面图或销售 deck 的 credibility 视觉。
场景：干净专业的版式环境，把产品、结果场景与轻量的数据可视化或证明元素结合起来。
主体：一个产品或服务场景，辅以案例卡片占位、认证感图形、结果指标或评价模块等背书元素。
构图：结构化且带编辑感，层级明确，让人一眼感到专业、可靠，并为 testimonial 文案或数据叠加留出空间。
光线：清晰、克制、偏商业与企业感的布光，不需要过度戏剧化。
材质与风格：现代 B2B / B2C 信任设计语言，配色克制，可读性强，以证据感而非装饰感为主。
约束：不要生成可读的伪评论，不要品牌 logo，不要信息图过载，不要水印。
```
