# Luxury Popup Experience

## Best For

- luxury retail activation
- popup campaign concept
- experiential brand visual

## Prompt (EN)

```text
Goal: Create a luxury popup installation visual that feels immersive, brand-building, and editorial rather than event-documentary.
Asset: Campaign concept image for a premium brand popup or retail activation.
Scene: Sculptural branded environment with architectural forms, dramatic lighting, premium materials, and a clear experiential focal point.
Subject: One luxury popup space designed around a signature product world, inviting exploration without showing a crowded event.
Composition: Wide but controlled composition, popup architecture dominant, strong spatial depth, balanced visual weight, not left-heavy.
Lighting: Cinematic retail lighting with dramatic highlights, glow accents, and believable shadow depth.
Materials and styling: Lacquer, brushed metal, stone, illuminated signage forms, quiet luxury palette, museum-like retail polish.
Constraints: No crowd chaos, no cheap trade-show booths, no readable third-party logos, no clutter, no watermark.
```

## 提示词（中文）

```text
目标：生成一张高奢 popup 装置视觉，让空间既有沉浸感，又像品牌建设级别的 campaign 图，而不是活动纪录照。
资产类型：高端品牌 popup 或零售激活项目的 campaign 概念图。
场景：带雕塑感的品牌空间，包含建筑化结构、戏剧布光、高级材质和明确的沉浸式焦点。
主体：一个围绕品牌核心产品世界打造的 luxury popup 空间，引导探索感，但不要出现拥挤活动现场。
构图：宽画幅但受控，popup 建筑结构占主导，空间纵深清晰，视觉重量平衡，不要左重右轻。
光线：电影感零售布光，带高光、发光边界和真实阴影深度。
材质与风格：漆面、拉丝金属、石材、发光 signage 结构、安静高奢配色、像博物馆级零售装置。
约束：不要人群混乱，不要廉价展会 booth 感，不要可读第三方 logo，不要杂乱，不要水印。
```
