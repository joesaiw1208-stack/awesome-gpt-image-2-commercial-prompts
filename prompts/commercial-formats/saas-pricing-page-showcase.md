# SaaS Pricing Page Showcase

## Best For

- pricing page mockup
- subscription plan visual
- SaaS conversion page concept

## Prompt (EN)

```text
Goal: Create a high-conversion SaaS pricing page screenshot that feels premium, trustworthy, and product-led.
Asset: Desktop pricing page mockup for a software website or campaign deck.
Scene: Structured pricing page with plan cards, feature comparison, testimonial strip, and clean CTA hierarchy.
Subject: One polished SaaS pricing experience designed to help visitors compare plans and upgrade confidently.
Composition: Tall centered desktop page with three or four plan cards as the focal system, balanced feature rows below, clean whitespace, no left-heavy weighting.
Lighting: Crisp interface lighting with subtle shadows and layered panel depth.
Materials and styling: Enterprise SaaS UI, glassy but restrained cards, soft blue or emerald accents, conversion-focused hierarchy, premium digital-product finish.
Constraints: No broken columns, no unreadable fine print, no startup-template clutter, no excessive badges, no watermark.
```

## 提示词（中文）

```text
目标：生成一张高转化的 SaaS 定价页截图，既要高级可信，又要体现产品导向和付费逻辑。
资产类型：软件官网定价页桌面端 mockup 或销售 deck 视觉。
场景：结构清晰的 pricing page，包含套餐卡片、功能对比、用户评价条和明确 CTA 层级。
主体：一个帮助访客比较套餐并做出升级决策的完整 SaaS 定价页体验。
构图：纵向居中的桌面页面，三到四张套餐卡片作为核心系统，下方配功能行和留白，不要左重右轻。
光线：清晰界面光感，带轻微阴影和层级深度。
材质与风格：企业级 SaaS UI、略带玻璃感但克制的卡片、柔和蓝色或祖母绿强调色、以转化为导向的层级、精致数字产品气质。
约束：不要栏位错乱，不要细小不可读文案，不要模板站杂乱感，不要过多标签贴片，不要水印。
```
