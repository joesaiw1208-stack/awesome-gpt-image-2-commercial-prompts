# Product Carousel Storyboard

## Best For

- carousel ad concept
- multi-product campaign system
- launch storyboard

## Prompt (EN)

```text
Goal: Create a commercial carousel-style storyboard that presents multiple products as one coherent campaign system.
Asset: Multi-slide social ad concept or product-carousel campaign visual.
Scene: A clean presentation board showing several adjacent campaign frames, each frame highlighting a different product angle, benefit, or styling moment.
Subject: A coordinated product family or product sequence designed to feel swipeable, narrative, and retail-ready.
Composition: Horizontal multi-panel layout with 3 to 5 aligned frames, each panel visually distinct but unified in palette and art direction.
Lighting: Consistent commercial lighting across frames so the full sequence feels like one campaign.
Materials and styling: Modern paid-social art direction, card-like framing, premium retail color discipline, clear product storytelling.
Constraints: No chaotic collage, no mismatched styles between panels, no tiny unreadable labels, no clutter, no watermark.
```

## 提示词（中文）

```text
目标：生成一张适合轮播广告或多帧 campaign 展示的商业故事板，让多个产品画面像同一套系统。
资产类型：多帧社媒广告概念图或产品轮播 campaign 视觉。
场景：一个干净的展示板，上面并列多个 campaign 画面，每一帧强调不同产品角度、卖点或造型时刻。
主体：一组协调统一的产品序列或产品家族，传达“可滑动、可叙事、可零售化”的完整视觉系统。
构图：横向多面板布局，包含 3 到 5 个对齐画面，每个分栏各有重点，但配色与 art direction 保持统一。
光线：所有分栏都保持一致的商业摄影光感，让整组视觉像同一场 campaign。
材质与风格：现代付费社媒 art direction、卡片式分镜、高级零售配色纪律、清晰的产品叙事。
约束：不要混乱拼贴，不要各栏风格不一致，不要细小不可读标签，不要杂乱，不要水印。
```
