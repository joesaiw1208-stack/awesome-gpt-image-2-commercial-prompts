# Holiday Gift Box Hero

## Best For

- gifting collection hero
- holiday sale campaign
- premium seasonal ad creative

## Prompt (EN)

```text
Goal: Create a premium holiday gifting image that feels elegant rather than noisy or kitschy.
Asset: Seasonal homepage hero or ad static.
Scene: Refined festive set with warm bokeh lights, deep evergreen accents, and restrained luxury styling.
Subject: One premium gift box with tasteful ribbon and no visible branding.
Composition: Centered hero image, generous negative space, strong retail readability, headline-safe layout.
Lighting: Warm cinematic glow with soft highlights and elevated festive mood.
Materials and styling: Premium paper, satin ribbon, matte box finish, deep green and gold palette.
Constraints: No people, no text, no logo, no clutter, no cheap holiday props, no watermark.
```

## 提示词（中文）

```text
目标：生成一张高级节日礼盒图，气质要优雅，不要喧闹或俗气。
资产类型：用于节日营销的首页 hero 或广告静态图。
场景：精致节庆场景，带暖色散景灯光、深绿色点缀和克制的奢华布置。
主体：一个高端礼盒作为唯一主角，带精致缎带，但不出现品牌元素。
构图：礼盒居中，留白充足，零售可读性强，适合叠加标题。
光线：温暖电影感光线，带柔和高光，整体节庆但高级。
材质与风格：高级纸材、缎带、哑光盒面，颜色以深绿和金色为主。
约束：不要人物，不要文字，不要 logo，不要杂乱，不要廉价节日道具，不要水印。
```

## Why It Works

Holiday creative performs better when it signals gifting value without looking like a discount flyer.
