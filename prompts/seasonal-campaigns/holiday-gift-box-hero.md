# Holiday Gift Box Hero

## Use Case

Holiday sale launch, gifting collection campaign, homepage hero, and social ad creative.

## Asset Type

Seasonal gifting campaign image

## Prompt

```text
Use case: product-mockup
Asset type: holiday gifting campaign hero
Primary request: Create a premium holiday e-commerce campaign image featuring a beautifully packaged gift box as the hero product.
Scene/backdrop: refined festive setting with soft warm bokeh lights, deep evergreen accents, and restrained luxe holiday styling.
Subject: one premium gift box with elegant wrapping details, tasteful ribbon, luxury gifting appeal, no visible branding.
Style/medium: high-end commercial seasonal product photography.
Composition/framing: centered hero image with generous negative space, clean commercial structure, suitable for headline overlay.
Lighting/mood: warm cinematic glow, festive, elegant, elevated rather than kitschy.
Color palette: deep green, gold, cream, warm light.
Materials/textures: premium paper, satin ribbon, matte box finish, subtle reflective highlights.
Text (verbatim): ""
Constraints: no people, no logo, no text, no clutter, no cheap holiday props, no watermark.
Avoid: cartoon holiday styling, discount-bin aesthetics, overcrowded festive decor.
```

## Why It Works

It gives seasonal campaigns a luxury tone instead of the usual noisy holiday-sales look.
