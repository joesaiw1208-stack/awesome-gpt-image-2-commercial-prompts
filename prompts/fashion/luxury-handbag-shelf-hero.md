# Luxury Handbag Shelf Hero

## Best For

- handbag PDP hero
- collection launch banner
- luxury retail static

## Prompt (EN)

```text
Goal: Create a high-end handbag hero image with strong shape definition and premium shelf presence.
Asset: E-commerce hero for a luxury handbag launch.
Scene: Warm neutral studio shelf setup with subtle architectural shadow and soft depth.
Subject: One structured leather handbag as the clear hero product.
Composition: Front three-quarter view, bag fully visible, clean breathing room, silhouette and handle shape easy to read.
Lighting: Soft studio light with controlled specular highlights and elegant shadow.
Materials and styling: Smooth leather, brushed metal hardware, muted taupe or black palette, luxury retail finish.
Constraints: No logo, no readable text, no model, no hands, no clutter, no extra bags, no deformation, no watermark.
```

## 提示词（中文）

```text
目标：生成一张高级手袋主视觉，让包型清晰、陈列感强、零售气质突出。
资产类型：奢侈手袋新品的电商 hero 图。
场景：暖中性色棚拍层板场景，带轻微建筑投影和柔和空间层次。
主体：一只结构感明确的皮革手袋作为唯一主角。
构图：正面偏三分之四视角，整只包完整可见，轮廓和提手形态清楚，四周有呼吸感。
光线：柔和棚拍光，金属件高光受控，阴影优雅。
材质与风格：顺滑皮革、拉丝金属五金、灰褐或黑色系，高级零售质感。
约束：不要 logo，不要可读文字，不要模特，不要手，不要杂乱，不要多余包袋，不要变形，不要水印。
```

## Why It Works

Luxury bag shoppers care about silhouette and finish, so the prompt keeps the background quiet and the form readable.
