# Editorial Fashion Flatlay

## Best For

- collection launch email
- category page hero
- social commerce flatlay

## Prompt (EN)

```text
Goal: Create a polished editorial flatlay for a fashion drop.
Asset: Collection launch visual for e-commerce and email.
Scene: Refined stone-gray textile backdrop with neat styling space and subtle texture.
Subject: One hero garment paired with a few complementary accessories, all commercially wearable.
Composition: Top-down flatlay, tidy magazine-style arrangement, product-first spacing, balanced negative space.
Lighting: Soft overhead light with crisp fabric detail and minimal harsh shadows.
Materials and styling: Cotton, linen, leather, brushed metal accents, neutral palette with one restrained accent color.
Constraints: No text, no logo, no messy styling, no unrelated props, no warped garments, no watermark.
```

## 提示词（中文）

```text
目标：生成一张适合时尚新品发布的高级 flatlay 平铺图。
资产类型：用于电商和邮件营销的系列上新视觉。
场景：带有细微纹理的石灰灰色布面背景，留出整洁陈列区域。
主体：一件核心服装搭配少量互补配饰，整体要日常可售卖。
构图：俯拍平铺，杂志级整洁摆放，产品优先，留白均衡。
光线：柔和顶光，突出面料细节，避免生硬阴影。
材质与风格：棉、亚麻、皮革、拉丝金属点缀，中性色为主，仅保留一个克制强调色。
约束：不要文字，不要 logo，不要凌乱摆放，不要无关道具，不要服装变形，不要水印。
```

## Why It Works

It stays practical for retail teams: clean styling, readable product hierarchy, and no distracting editorial excess.
