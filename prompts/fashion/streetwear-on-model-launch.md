# Streetwear On-Model Launch

## Use Case

Streetwear drop campaign visual for homepage hero use, Instagram ads, and collection launch banners.

## Asset Type

On-model fashion campaign image

## Prompt

```text
Use case: photorealistic-natural
Asset type: streetwear launch campaign image
Primary request: Create a premium streetwear campaign image featuring one model wearing the hero outfit from a new collection drop.
Scene/backdrop: clean urban architectural backdrop with concrete, steel, and minimal city texture, uncluttered.
Subject: one confident model wearing a coordinated streetwear look with the outfit clearly visible and commercially desirable.
Style/medium: fashion campaign photography for a modern premium streetwear label.
Composition/framing: mid-length fashion crop, strong silhouette readability, campaign-ready posture, negative space for headline placement.
Lighting/mood: natural daylight with polished editorial contrast, cool, sharp, aspirational.
Color palette: charcoal, concrete gray, muted neutrals, one strategic accent color.
Materials/textures: cotton fleece, nylon, denim, technical fabric, architectural concrete.
Text (verbatim): ""
Constraints: no logo, no crowd, no text, no chaotic street clutter, no distorted body proportions.
Avoid: fast-fashion influencer look, low-end catalog pose, excessive props.
```

## Why It Works

It keeps the energy of fashion campaigns while preserving outfit readability for commerce use.
