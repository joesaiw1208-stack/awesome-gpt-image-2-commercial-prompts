# Boot Craftsmanship Vertical Launch

## Best For

- vertical social ad
- craftsmanship campaign
- premium footwear launch

## Prompt (EN)

```text
Goal: Create a vertical footwear campaign image that highlights craftsmanship, materials, and premium brand confidence.
Asset: 9:16 or 4:5 launch creative for a boot brand awareness campaign.
Scene: Tall immersive composition with a handcrafted leather boot in a moody workshop-meets-editorial environment, suggesting process and quality without turning into documentary clutter.
Subject: One premium leather boot as the hero product, with visible stitching, sole construction, and rich material texture.
Composition: Vertical hero frame, boot dominant, enough top and bottom breathing room for campaign use, strong silhouette, ad-ready balance.
Lighting: Directional low-key light with warm highlights on leather grain and subtle industrial shadow depth.
Materials and styling: Full-grain leather, stacked sole texture, brushed hardware, wood or metal workshop cues, rugged-luxury mood.
Constraints: No person, no hand, no logo, no text, no tool chaos, no muddy background, no cheap workwear catalog look, no watermark.
```

## 提示词（中文）

```text
目标：生成一张强调工艺、材质和品牌自信感的竖版鞋履 campaign 图。
资产类型：适用于 9:16 或 4:5 的品牌曝光广告、鞋靴发布图。
场景：高挑沉浸式构图，一只手工感皮靴置于“工坊感与编辑感结合”的环境中，暗示工艺过程但不过度纪录片化。
主体：一只高端皮靴作为绝对主角，缝线、鞋底结构和皮革纹理必须清晰可信。
构图：竖版 hero 构图，鞋靴占主导，顶部和底部保留足够呼吸空间，轮廓明确，适合广告裁切。
光线：低调方向性布光，暖色高光掠过皮革纹理，环境阴影带轻微工业层次。
材质与风格：全粒面皮革、鞋底叠层纹理、拉丝金属件、木质或金属工坊线索，整体是 rugged luxury 气质。
约束：不要人物，不要手，不要 logo，不要文字，不要工具杂乱，不要脏乱背景，不要廉价工装目录感，不要水印。
```
