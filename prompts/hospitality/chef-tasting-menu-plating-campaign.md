# Chef Tasting Menu Plating Campaign

## Best For

- fine-dining campaign
- tasting menu launch visual
- restaurant social hero

## Prompt (EN)

```text
Goal: Create a fine-dining campaign image that feels precise, elevated, and editorial.
Asset: Commercial visual for a chef tasting-menu launch.
Scene: Dark intimate restaurant table with one refined place setting and controlled background blur.
Subject: One meticulously plated signature dish as the hero object, presented with modern fine-dining restraint.
Composition: Slightly above eye level, plate dominant in frame, negative space around the dish, premium magazine-style balance.
Lighting: Focused warm spotlight with soft falloff, preserving texture and dimensional plating details.
Materials and styling: Ceramic plate, refined garnish, brushed cutlery, linen texture, upscale dining atmosphere.
Constraints: No readable menu text, no visible brand marks, no extra dishes, no crowded table, no messy sauce splatter, no watermark.
```

## 提示词（中文）

```text
目标：生成一张高级餐饮 campaign 图，画面要精准、克制并带有编辑感。
资产类型：主厨 tasting menu 发布的商业视觉。
场景：昏暗而亲密的餐厅桌面，一套餐具陈设，背景轻微虚化。
主体：一道精心摆盘的招牌菜作为唯一主角，呈现现代 fine dining 的克制感。
构图：略高于平视角度，餐盘在画面中占主导，周围留白，像高级杂志拍摄。
光线：聚焦式暖光，边缘柔和下沉，同时保留食材纹理和立体摆盘细节。
材质与风格：陶瓷餐盘、精致点缀、拉丝餐具、亚麻桌布、整体高端餐饮氛围。
约束：不要可读菜单文字，不要品牌标识，不要多道菜，不要拥挤桌面，不要凌乱酱汁飞溅，不要水印。
```

## Why It Works

It treats plating as a commercial subject, which helps the output feel like restaurant branding rather than casual food photography.
